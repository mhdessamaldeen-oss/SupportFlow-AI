# Incident Resolution Checklist

## Similar-case review

Before recommending a new fix, review resolved tickets with the same product area, environment, and external system. Reuse prior resolution steps when the root cause and operational context align.

## Evidence-backed next step

The next support action should be a short operational step such as verify access configuration, confirm integration credentials, reproduce in the reported browser, or validate the latest deployment state. Recommendations should stay grounded in available evidence.

## Weak evidence handling

When supporting evidence is limited, state that confidence is weak and direct the team to gather one more validating signal, such as logs, a screenshot, or confirmation of the affected user group.
